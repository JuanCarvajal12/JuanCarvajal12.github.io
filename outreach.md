Most of my outreach work has been through **RECA** ([Red de Estudiantes Colombianos en Astronomía](https://www.astroreca.org/en)), specifically the *nodo Educación*. It’s a volunteer-driven effort that supports astronomy education across Colombia.

[RECA Educación](https://www.astroreca.org/en/educacion) runs several projects that offer free activities for schools and cultural centers. I have contributed to a few of them, but my main focus has been **[BARCo (Bringing Astronomy to Rural Communities of Colombia)](https://www.astroreca.org/en/barco-astronomia-rural-colombia)**, a project designed for rural schools where access to lab infrastructure and, especially, reliable internet can be limited.

---

## BARCo: Bringing Astronomy to Rural Communities of Colombia

BARCo provides **ready-to-use classroom activities** designed by astronomers, packaged as an offline-friendly **kit** with teacher-facing guides. The emphasis is hands-on and realistic: activities that can be run in real classrooms with limited resources. We made the materials openly available so they can be replicated elsewhere ([BARCo kit](https://www.astroreca.org/en/barco-astronomia-rural-colombia/kit-barco) · [Zenodo record](https://zenodo.org/records/13738441)).

The project was conceptualized in 2023 and implemented in 2024 with support from the **IAU Office of Astronomy for Development**. Most schools continued receiving support from RECA Educación in 2025.

BARCo was built to reach communities that are often missed by “standard” outreach formats. In many rural regions of Colombia, everyday constraints go well beyond “no internet”: there are long-standing infrastructure gaps, and some communities have been historically affected by violence and insecurity. On top of that, many of these regions often feel forgotten by the central government. This project was an attempt to contribute through education to the development of children and communities of these areas. 

I contributed as a volunteer mainly by helping with the design of activities, helping write and edit the booklets, contacting schools and providing support to teachers.
